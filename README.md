# 📊 Sales Analysis for an E-commerce Business

## Project Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce sales data to identify key trends, top-performing categories, regional performance, and seasonal patterns. The goal is to extract business insights that can support decision-making.

---

## Dataset
The dataset contains 34,500 sales transactions and 17 features, including:
- Product category
- Price and quantity
- Total sales amount
- Region
- Discount and profit margin
- Order date

---

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Google Colab

---

## Key Insights
- Electronics is the top-performing category, generating the highest revenue.
- Sales are strong across all regions, with the South region performing slightly better.
- April and December show peak sales, indicating seasonal trends.
- Sales remain relatively stable throughout the year.

---

## Business Recommendations
- Focus on Electronics category for revenue growth.
- Increase marketing efforts before peak months (April & December).
- Expand strategy in high-performing regions like the South.
- Improve performance of low-performing categories.

---

## Conclusion
The analysis highlights the importance of category focus, seasonal planning, and regional strategies. These insights can help businesses improve decision-making and increase revenue.
